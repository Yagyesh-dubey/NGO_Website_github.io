About Our Organisation

We the group of 5 students from 2nd year created a NGO website naming SPREADING SMILES (SS) for the 1 credit course PROJECT EXHIBITION under the supervision of Dr. Ravi verma (Sr. Asst. Prof.) sir which is based on the motive to help poor people on the basis of their needs like in terms of food , education , agriculture etc. The website includes the a deep understanding and use of front-end and back-end development technologies .

The technologies which we have used in the development of this website in front-end are HTML5 , CSS3 , Bootstrap ,Tailwind , JS , j Query , React JS .
And for back-end our used technologies are PHP , MYSQL only .

Organisational structure

SPREADING SMILES believes in multiplying its efforts through collaboration & partnerships that bring together government, non-government, and corporate in a collaborative process for socio-economic development, improving agriculture, environment, health, and women empowerment. SSF puts a lot of emphasis on upgrading the skills and capacities of its staff. For this purpose, need-based training is regularly organized

Vision

We aim to create a development revolution for the marginalized and socio-economically weaker sections of society.

Mission

Our mission is the creation of such a society where nobody is deprived of the main current of development. We are determined for the establishment of that society in which everybody possesses ‘Human rights’ with dignity.
